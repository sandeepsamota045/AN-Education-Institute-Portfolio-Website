General Facilities

Canteen

Vivekanand Defence Academy has a spacious canteen on its campus. Situated in a clean, well maintained atmosphere the canteen is open from 8.00 am to 6.30 pm.It is open for all and there is a separate seating arrangement for staff, students and patients.

The management is fully committed to keep the qualityof the canteen for the utmost benefit of all concerned.

 

Stationary Stores

All stationary items are made available in the stores at reasonable rates, fixed by the college management.

 

24x7 Medical Support

If your ward require any sort of Medical Aid like Stomach Ache, Giddiness, Faint or any other Physical Problem. Our 24 Hrs. Medical Help is available for the students of Vivekanand Defence Academy. If any student needs to go to hospital he is accompanied by one of the wardens working in specific shift.

 

RO Water

The entire campus is facilitated with pure Reverse Osmosis (RO) drinking water with water coolers in every block to cater to the need of pure and safe drinking water to all. A high level of maintenance attention and regular testing ensure the quality of the water. The maximum capacity of the plant is 1000 litres per hour. The rejects water from the plant is used for gardening and washing purposes.

 

Waiting Hall : Here the visitors, parents and other outsiders are allowed to wait.

 

CCTV Camera Security.

Cool drinking water facility during summers.

Solar Geyser facility during winters.

24 hrs. Electricity Backup.

Fire Extinguishers.
